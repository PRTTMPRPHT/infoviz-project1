Controls
  • Click on columns in the heatmap to select and unselect group members.
  • Hover over columns in the heatmap to get a preview of their skill profile.
  • Click on skill labels in the heatmap to re-sort the heatmap.
  • Drag the ends of the slider on the right to change highlighting of the heatmap.
